Our Initial Response Plan
  1. Medical & Hygiene Assistance
    Basic first aid, hygiene kits, and medicine will be provided to prevent illness and infections.
  2. Identify Affected Families
    We are working with local volunteers to identify the most severely affected families, especially children, elderly people, and those who lost their homes.
  3. Provide Emergency Essentials
    We will distribute food packs, clean drinking water, basic clothing, and essential medicines to meet urgent needs.
  4. Temporary Shelter Support
    Families without shelter will receive support for temporary accommodation, including tarpaulins, mats, and blankets.
  5. Ongoing Monitoring & Updates
    We will share regular updates on fund usage and expand support based on community needs.
